What do you want to know

E Sarthi is an innovative initiative dedicated to addressing local transportation challenges in our district.

Our goal is to identify individuals capable of making a Rs. 20,000 down payment to own an e-rickshaw. The remaining amount of Rs. 1,50,000 will be financed through a 24-month installment plan at a competitive interest rate of 16%.

Read More

Out Objective

  • Enhance local transportation accessibility
  • Empower individuals economically
  • Promote environmental sustainability through the use of eco-friendly e-rickshaws
Monthly Installments:
Participants in the E Sarthi program will start paying a monthly installment of Rs. 7,250 from February 1, 2024, continuing each month for 24 months.
Benefits for Participants
Affordable Ownership
Modest down payment and manageable monthly installments enable individuals to become proud owners of e-rickshaws.
Income Generation
E-rickshaws provide a sustainable source of income through local transportation services, contributing to financial stability.
Environmental Impact
Promoting electric vehicles helps reduce the carbon footprint and fosters environmental sustainability.
